About Me

Again, thank you for visiting my website. I went to South Newton High School and took general courses there but that is where I found my love for Computer Graphics (CGT). After high school, I attended Purdue University and got my Bachelor's of Science in Computer Graphic Technology (CGT). While I was attending Purdue University I decided to get a minor which was in Computer Network and Information Technology (CNIT). After college I got an employment opportunity down in Atlanta, Georgia to do iOS Development. I spent three months in Atlanta and developed two complete applications while down there. The two applications are in the App Store. I would like to stay local but still continue my pursue of iOS Development.
